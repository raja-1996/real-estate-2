# Broker Pre-Visit Questionnaire (Bangalore)

Use this before agreeing to a site visit. The goals are:

1. **Share my requirements upfront** so the broker filters the right inventory.
2. **Qualify each property** against the appreciation and due-diligence factors in the [README](./README.md).
3. **Save both our time** — no driving across town for a B-khata flat with no Cauvery line.

> Send the **My Requirements** section to the broker over WhatsApp/email. Ask the **Per-Property** questions for every shortlist before the visit.

---

## Part A — My Requirements (share with broker)

- **Down payment ready**: ₹____

- **Negotiation buffer**: ___ % over listed price acceptable

- [ ] Villa / row house
- [ ] Plot — DTCP / BMRDA / BBMP, min size ___ sqft
- [ ] Independent house
- **Carpet area floor**: ___ sqft (not super built-up)
- **Floor preference**: low / mid / high — avoid ground & top: yes / no
- **Facing**: E / N / NE preferred — avoid W-facing without shade

### A4. Location filters (per README tiers)
- **Tiers I will consider**: ___ (e.g., Tier 3 + Tier 4)
- **Micro-markets shortlisted**: ____, ____, ____
- **Hard exclusions**: flood-prone (Bellandur/Varthur/Bommanahalli inner), B-khata zones, gram-panchayat plots without DC conversion
- **Max commute** to ____ (office/school) at peak hour: ___ minutes
- **Max distance** to nearest operational metro: ___ km
- **Max distance** to nearest tech park (ORR / Whitefield / E-City / Manyata): ___ km

### A5. Project & builder

- **Project age** (for resale): max ___ years

### A6. Title & approvals (deal-breakers)
- A-khata only — **B-khata: hard no**
- Clear EC for last 30 years
- Sanctioned plan from BBMP / BDA / BMRDA (specify which jurisdiction is acceptable)

### A7. Utilities & infrastructure (deal-breakers)

### A8. Social infra proximity

- Hospital tier-1 (Manipal/Apollo/Narayana/Aster) within ___ km
- Daily-needs (supermarket, pharmacy) within walking distance

### A9. Logistics
- Available visit windows: weekday eve / Sat AM / Sun
- Will visit **max 3 properties per outing** — pre-screen accordingly
- Need **soft copies before visit**: brochure, price sheet (with all charges broken out), floor plan, RERA ID, sample agreement, EC, khata, OC

## Part B — Per-Property Qualifying Questions (ask before each visit)

Run through this list for each shortlist. If the broker can't answer 80% of these confidently, skip the visit.

### B1. Pricing transparency
1. What is the **base price per sqft on carpet area** (not super built-up)?
2. Full breakup: base + floor rise + PLC + parking + club + GST + stamp duty + registration + BBMP khata + corpus + maintenance advance + brokerage. **Total all-in?**
3. Is the price **negotiable**? Typical closing discount in this project?
4. Last 3 actual transaction prices in this project (with month) — not asking price.
5. How does this compare to ₹/sqft for the **same micro-market 12 months ago**?

### B2. Title & legal
6. **Khata type**: A or B? If B, what's the conversion path and timeline?
7. Jurisdiction: BBMP / BDA / BMRDA / gram panchayat?
8. **RERA ID** — share the link.
9. **OC and CC** — issued? Date?
10. **Encumbrance Certificate** — clean for 30 years? Any mortgage on the unit?
11. Any **pending litigation** on the project, land, or builder?
12. For plots: **DC conversion** order number, RTC, mutation status.

### B3. Builder & project health
13. Builder's **last 3 delivered projects** in Bangalore — names, possession dates vs promised.
14. This project: **% sold, % occupied, current inventory**.
15. **Construction-linked vs subvention** payment plan?
16. Any **delays** in this phase? Revised possession date?
17. **Defect liability period** and what's covered.

### B4. Appreciation drivers (per README factors)
18. Distance to nearest **operational metro** station (km, walking time).
19. Distance to nearest **under-construction metro** + expected commissioning.
20. Distance to nearest **tech park cluster** (name it).
21. Any **announced infrastructure** within 3 km — flyover, road widening, PRR/STRR, signal-free corridor?
22. Within **airport corridor** influence zone? (relevant for North Bangalore)
23. **Inventory months** in this micro-market (rough estimate)?
24. Recent **comparable resale** price within 1 km — appreciation over last 3 years?

### B5. Utilities & risk factors
25. Water source: **Cauvery line live**, or borewell + tanker? Monthly water bill?
26. **Flood history**: any waterlogging in 2022 monsoon? Lake within 500m — which one?
27. Sewage: STP capacity vs current load, UGD connection?
28. Power: sanctioned load per unit, DG backup KVA per unit, common-area backup?
29. **Soil/structural**: any settlement complaints? Borewell on plot or external?

### B6. Unit-specific
30. **Carpet / built-up / super built-up** breakup — exact sqft.
31. **Loading factor** (%) — should be 25–35%.
32. **Vastu**: facing, main door direction, kitchen position.
33. Floor plan — any **dead walls / wasted space**?
34. **Common wall** with neighbor or lift shaft? (noise)
35. Natural light + cross-ventilation — which rooms?
36. **Parking**: covered / open, allotted bay number, EV charging?
37. **Modifications allowed** post-handover (false ceiling, modular kitchen, etc.)?

### B7. Maintenance & community
38. **Monthly maintenance** ₹/sqft and what it covers.
39. **Sinking fund / corpus** balance and recent major spends.
40. **Association** formed? Builder still in control?
41. Tenant-to-owner ratio (relevant for resale and community feel).
42. Pet policy, short-term-rental (Airbnb) policy.

### B8. Resale & exit
43. How long does a resale unit here typically take to sell?
44. Last resale: **listed price vs closed price**, days on market.
45. Any **lock-in or transfer charges** from builder for first resale?

### B9. Visit logistics
46. Will the **owner / authorized builder rep** be present? (not just broker)
47. Can I bring my own **civil engineer / lawyer** for inspection?
48. Can I see **multiple units** (different floors/facings) on the same visit?
49. **Token amount** if I like it — refundable? Cooling-off period?
50. **Brokerage**: % and at what stage paid?

## Part C — Red Flags → Cancel the Visit

If any of these come up in broker's answers, don't waste the trip:

- B-khata, gram-panchayat plot without DC conversion, no RERA ID
- "OC will come soon" for a property occupied >6 months
- Refuses to share EC, sanctioned plan, or last transaction prices
- Builder has >2 delayed projects in last 5 years
- Lake within 200m on the downstream side (check BBMP flood map)
- Borewell-only with no Cauvery roadmap in a >3-year-old project
- Pricing >15% above the micro-market median without a clear premium justification (lake-facing, top floor, corner unit)
- "Cash component" required for any part of the deal
